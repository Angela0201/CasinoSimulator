# Casino Simulator

This project is a mini game where you can play simple games of chances, win bonuses, manage your coin balance, and view statistics.

---

## Home

The Home screen displays:
- current coin balance;
- total coins earned and lost for each game.

---

## Games

In this section you can choose one of three games:
- Dice;
- Coin Flip;
- Slots.

---

## Dice

- Choose a number from 1 to 6 and place a bet.
- If the number matches, you win.
- If the dice rolls 6, a jackpot is activated.
- The result immediately affects your balance, stats, and is saved to history.

---

## Slots

- Place a bet and spin the reels.
- Matching symbols bring winnings.
- Diamonds give jackpots.

---

## Coin Flip

- Place a bet and tap the coin.
- On a win, the bet is added to the balance.
- Each consecutive win increases the multiplier based on the win streak number.
- On a loss, the win streak is reset.

---

## History

The History screen shows:
- all played games;
- bet amounts;
- win or loss amounts (including jackpots);
- time of each game.

---

## Settings

In Settings you can:
- switch between light and dark themes;
- change text size;
- reset the balance;
- clear game history (including statistics).
